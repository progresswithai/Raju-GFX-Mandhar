Frequently Asked Questions

What creative services does Raju GFX offer?

Raju GFX is a premium post-production and design studio based in Mandhar, Raipur. We specialize in high-end cinematic video editing (weddings, events, and commercial ads), social media content optimization (Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts), high-fidelity UI/UX wedding album designs, and professional brand logo development.

What is the typical turnaround time for a video project?

Turnaround times vary based on the complexity and scale of the footage. High-retention social media reels or promotional teasers are typically delivered within 2 to 4 days. Complex, long-form cinematic wedding highlights or commercial documentaries require a detailed multi-week process involving meticulous sound syncing, pacing adjustment, and color grading.

What do I need to provide to start a video or design project?
To kick off your project, you will need to provide your raw footage or design assets, a brief overview of your visual style preferences, and any specific text details or audio clips you want featured. For custom branding or interface work, sharing your color goals and references helps us maintain a pixel-perfect match with your brand's vision.
How do I share large raw video files with your team?

You can seamlessly share large raw files with us using cloud storage networks like Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer. If you are operating locally in or around Mandhar, Raipur, you can also drop off your raw project footage directly on an external hard drive or high-speed storage device at our studio.

What software and industry tools do you use for production?

We handle post-production workflows using industry-standard professional platforms to secure elite quality outputs. Video projects are cut, colored, and mixed inside Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects, while our design assets, branding layouts, and UI/UX custom wireframes are engineered using Figma and vector tools.
